Procedural Snow Generation

In this project, I developed a procedural snow generation system aimed at creating realistic snowscapes in virtual environments. The goal was to simulate natural snow deposition processes to enhance the visual fidelity of snow-covered landscapes in real-time applications, such as video games or virtual simulations. The Snow could also be chnaged and edited and changed so that it can work for whoever needs the tool.

Nano Arena

NanoArena is an engaging wave survival game set in a visually striking, Tron-like arena. Players must demonstrate their combat skills and strategic prowess as they face off against two types of AI adversaries: a fast and relentless melee attacker, and a precise, ranged shooter. Each wave intensifies the challenge, requiring players to adapt and refine their tactics to survive. The sleek, futuristic design of NanoArena, combined with its dynamic gameplay, offers a thrilling and immersive experience for gamers seeking both action and strategy.

MAGEKEEP

MageKeep is a strategic tower defense game set in a mystical fantasy world. Players must protect their city from waves of fearsome dragons by strategically placing magical towers along a predetermined path. With procedural content generation, each playthrough offers a unique and challenging experience, keeping players engaged and testing their strategic skills.
